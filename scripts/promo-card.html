⚙️  How It Works
  • 📄 Tails your EQ log from end-of-file — never reads your multi-GB history
  • 🚫 Strips private channels locally, before any parsing or upload
  • 📤 Uploads compact JSON per fight — just combat events, nothing else
  • 🤖 Bot posts the card automatically on boss death
  • 🤝 Multi-perspective merge — more parsers = more complete picture

The more parsers running, the better the picture. Every perspective fills in damage events, heal targets, and death timing that others may have missed. If only one healer's running the agent, the card only shows what that one log saw. Everyone running it means complete CH chain data, complete DPS, complete death tracking.
▶  One command — runs alongside EQ in a terminal
$ node wolfpack-logsync --log "C:\EverQuest\Logs\eqlog_YourName_pq.proj.txt" --watch
